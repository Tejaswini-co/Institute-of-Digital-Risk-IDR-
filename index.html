Industry-led Institute for the Digital Era

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led institute focusing on resolving the talent gap in digital risk, cyber risk, and AI governance.

In partnership with leading UK universities, we deliver specialised training, integrating real-world projects with industry practices to engineer resilient technology ecosystems.

Our Pillars

IDR operates across three core pillars designed to train talent, develop innovation, and support organisations in implementing modern digital risk frameworks.

📚

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps for students and professionals in digital and cyber risk.

💡

Innovation & Incubation

Developing intellectual property, AI governance models, and next-generation risk frameworks.

🛡️

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.
